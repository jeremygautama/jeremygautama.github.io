Abby (2020)

During the COVID-19 pandemic, a young man experiences a psychological breakdown after receiving an unprecedented text from his crush

This movie was inspired by my real-life experience with someone.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, I stumbled upon the Duplass brothers’ This is John. It felt like a religious experience coming together. Not only did it got into Sundance, but it only cost them $3 to make. I want to do that. But here’s the catch: I have no friends to help, nor do I have money to budget.

So what was my plan? To make the film by myself and budgeting it with $0.

The filming process went on for about a week, the first day being the idea of making the film. I don’t know what happened, but that exact day, I instantly took my phone and recorded myself acting on something. I don't actually know what I was doing, but that's okay (I thought to myself). The story went on from being shots of hollowness, to becoming a solid story.

I think it's a nice side-project to fill up my boredom during these weird times.

“Don’t write what you think people want to read. Find your voice and write about what’s in your heart.” - Quentin Tarantino
